Progressive Equity Calculator

This tool will estimate payouts for equity holders under a progressive
equity scheme. Progressive equity is a legal structure that allows employees
to accrue a larger share of the funds from an exit once the founders and
other large equity holders, such as early employees, have reached a predetermined
"financial independence" number. Once this number has been reached, equity holders
are taxed 50% (or whatever you specify) of their payout earnings and that amount
is put into a pool that is then shared by the rest of the employees. Investors,
of course, are not subject to progressive equity.
